# Playground

Welcome to the sandbox.

This repository is home to experiments, proofs of concept, learning projects, and the occasional rabbit hole that seemed like a good idea at the time.

Some projects here are early explorations of technologies, patterns, or architectural ideas. Others exist purely because I was curious and wanted to see how something worked. A few may eventually graduate into my main portfolio. Many will not. That's perfectly fine.

Think of this repository as the space between:

* "This might become something useful."
* "I wonder what happens if I try this."
* "Well, now I need to know."

## What You'll Find

* Technology evaluations
* Platform and infrastructure experiments
* Small applications and prototypes
* Learning exercises
* Architectural explorations
* The occasional questionable decision made in the name of curiosity

## What You Won't Find

* Production-ready guarantees
* Long-term support commitments
* A promise that every project is finished
* Evidence that every experiment was a good idea

## Why This Exists

As engineers, we spend much of our careers standing on layers of abstraction built by someone else. Every now and then it's worth peeling back a layer to understand what lies beneath.

This repository is where I do that.

Sometimes I discover a useful pattern. Sometimes I discover why the abstraction exists in the first place.

Either way, I learn something.

Happy exploring.
